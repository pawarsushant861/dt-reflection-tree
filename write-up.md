# Design Rationale

## Why these questions
I designed questions that feel natural after a workday. Each question reflects real internal dialogue.

## Axis Design
Axis 1: Focuses on control vs reaction
Axis 2: Shifts toward contribution vs expectation
Axis 3: Expands perspective toward others

## Branching Logic
Used decision nodes to split based on answers instead of scoring models.

## Psychology Used
- Locus of Control (Rotter)
- Growth Mindset (Dweck)
- Organizational Citizenship Behavior
- Self-Transcendence (Maslow)
